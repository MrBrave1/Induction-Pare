Roles and Responsibilities

General Responsibilities

Responsibilities of the Project Manager

  • Responsible for the execution of the project and accountable for all HSE matters.
  • Provides the necessary resources to ensure HSE objectives are achieved.
  • Leads project coordination with customers, consulting engineers, and consortium partners.
  • Oversees implementation of the Health, Safety, and Environmental Management System at the project site.

Responsibilities of the Site Manager

  • Has overall responsibility for all work carried out on site in accordance with contract requirements.
  • Ensures the Health, Safety, and Environmental Management System is effectively implemented.
  • Maintains and updates the Health, Safety, and Environmental Plan as work progresses.
  • Ensures arrangements for site security, emergency preparedness, and safety coordination are in place.
  • Monitors health, safety, and environmental protection measures regularly.

Responsibilities of Supervisors and Contractors

  • Identify workplace risks and implement appropriate control measures.
  • Ensure work is carried out safely and in accordance with instructions.
  • Maintain equipment and materials in safe working condition.
  • Conduct toolbox talks and safety briefings when required.
  • Report accidents, incidents, unsafe conditions, and equipment defects.
  • Participate in health, safety, and environmental meetings and activities.
  • Comply with all additional safety requirements communicated by site management.

Responsibilities of All Personnel on Site

  • Comply with health, safety, and environmental requirements.
  • Use personal protective equipment (PPE) as provided and instructed.
  • Report hazards, deficiencies, incidents, and accidents immediately.
  • Take responsibility for personal safety and the safety of others.

Responsibilities of the HSE Advisor

  • Assist in the implementation and updating of the HSE Plan.
  • Support risk assessments and development of control measures.
  • Conduct and record regular safety inspections and audits.
  • Investigate and document accidents and incidents.
  • Coordinate health, safety, and environmental aspects of site activities.
  • Organize induction programs and site-specific HSE training.

Remember: Safety is everyone's responsibility. Always work safely, follow procedures, and report unsafe conditions immediately.

Emergency Escape Routes in HEP

Mandatory
  • Safety Helmet, Safety Shoes, and Reflective Jacket are mandatory while on site.
  • COVID safety measures shall be followed at all times.
  • Only authorized site vehicles are permitted to enter the powerhouse.
  • Entry without a valid ID card is strictly prohibited.
  • Visitors must be accompanied by the concerned authorized person at all times.
  • By signing this document, you agree to comply with all site safety rules and instructions.
Do's
  • If you are a Doctor or First Aider, please inform the Site Manager/HSE Advisor. Your assistance may be required during emergencies.
  • Follow all safety instructions and warning signs displayed in the work areas.
  • Use appropriate PPE as required for the area being visited.
  • Watch your step and avoid tripping over materials, tools, or other objects.
  • Remain alert and be aware of your surroundings at all times.
  • If you hear an emergency hooter or siren, follow the designated emergency exit signs and proceed to the assembly point. Stay calm, do not panic, and wait for further instructions.
  • If you observe any near miss, accident, incident, unsafe act, or unsafe condition, report it immediately to the HSE Advisor or Site Manager.
Don'ts
  • Do not enter the powerhouse unless authorized by Security.
  • Do not enter excavations, confined spaces (such as pipes, inlets, or manholes), or approach hazardous materials without proper authorization and training.
  • Do not enter any unauthorized or restricted area.
  • Do not touch or operate any equipment, materials, or chemicals unless authorized to do so.
  • Do not carry cameras, laptops, storage devices, CDs, or other recording/media devices into the powerhouse without prior permission.
  • Smoking is prohibited within the site premises except in designated smoking areas.
  • Consumption of tobacco, gutka, or similar products is prohibited within the site premises.
  • Never touch any component, material, or chemical that may present hot surface, sharp edge, or chemical exposure hazards.
  • Do not work or stand near civil construction activities or excavated areas. Maintain a safe distance from construction equipment and moving vehicles.
